Sangsoo Jun – Candidate for Secretary General, Inter-Parliamentary Union (IPU)

“Connecting Parliaments through Innovation”

My Vision

I am honored to submit my candidacy for Secretary General of the Inter-Parliamentary Union (IPU). With a lifelong commitment to democratic governance, I bring leadership experience and institutional insight to advance the IPU’s mission.

My decision to run was shaped by a grave incident at the Korean National Assembly, reaffirming my resolve to defend democracy globally. If elected, I will pursue three core missions:

  • Elevate the IPU as a Global Platform for Democracy
  • Champion AI-based Digital Innovation for Inclusive Representation
  • Lead Strategic Reforms for the IPU’s Sustainability and Institutional Resilience

Core Missions

1. Elevate the IPU as a Global Platform for Democratic Dialogue

Strengthen parliamentary voices, reinforce democratic institutions, and promote gender equality, youth engagement, and diversity.

2. Champion AI-based Digital Innovation

Promote e-parliament systems, equitable access to digital tools, and AI-powered legislative technology.

3. Lead Strategic Reforms

Revitalize the IPU by broadening its financial base, expanding membership, and enhancing transparency and efficiency.
